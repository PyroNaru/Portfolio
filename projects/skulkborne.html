SkulkBorne

Description

This is a modification inspired by the works of Lovecraft and games such as Bloodborne and Darkwood and seeks to bring this madness and horror into Minecraft. Inspired by the likes of Miskatonic Mysteries and Bewitchment, Skulkborne aims to bring custom weapons, magical rituals, and fearsome enemies into the world of Minecraft.

The Mod is very branched out with different routes to explore with two major paths

  • Hunters, Mysterious Group who specializes in killing beasts and dabbling with alchemical formulas
  • Cultist, A group dedicated to summoning the ancient ones and dabbling with occult magic.

Features

  • Custom Weapons & Tools - Forge new armaments inspired by gothic and eldritch designs.
  • Magic & Rituals - Experiment with forbidden spells and summon eldritch entities.
  • Terrifying Enemies - Face beasts, cultists, and cosmic horrors unlike anything in vanilla Minecraft.
  • Faction System - Join the Hunters or Cultists, each with unique gameplay.
  • Branching Exploration - Multiple paths, hidden secrets, and choices that shape your adventure.
  • Atmospheric World - Dark forests, cursed ruins, and unsettling environments to immerse players in a Lovecraftian setting.
