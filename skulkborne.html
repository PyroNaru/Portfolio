SkulkBorne

Description

This is a modification inspired by the works of Lovecraft and games such as Bloodborne and Darkwood and seeks to bring this madness and horror into Minecraft. Inspired by the likes of Miskatonic Mysteries and Bewitchment, Skulkborne aims to bring custom weapons, magic, enemies, and threat into the world of Minecraft.
